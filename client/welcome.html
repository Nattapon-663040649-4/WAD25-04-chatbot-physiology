Recomended Topics

Nervous System

The nervous system is the body's command center and communication network, made up of a complex arrangement of nerve cells called neurons. Its main function is to receive, process, and respond to sensory information, allowing for everything from basic bodily functions (like breathing and heart rate) to complex actions (like movement, thought, and memory).

Circulatory System

The circulatory system, also called the cardiovascular system, is the body's transportation network. Its primary function is to circulate blood to deliver oxygen and nutrients to all cells and tissues while simultaneously removing carbon dioxide and waste products.
